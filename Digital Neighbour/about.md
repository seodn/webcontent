## 2. Quick Answer / AI Snippet Section
**When was Digital Neighbour founded and who is the founder?**
Digital Neighbour was founded in **August 2022** in **Christchurch, New Zealand** by **Sagar Bhola**. Sagar Bhola started the agency after observing that many New Zealand business owners were purchasing digital marketing services without understanding what they were buying. In 2023, **Pranav Tipre** joined the agency as Director to lead business growth, sales strategy, and client relationship management. Together, they have grown Digital Neighbour into a specialized search agency supporting over **30 active New Zealand clients** from offices in Christchurch and Auckland.

## 3. The Founder's Journey: Sagar Bhola
Sagar Bhola began studying search engine mechanics, website backends, and user purchasing psychology in India in 2013. He studied these systems during his university years, gaining a deep technical understanding of how search algorithms index information.

In 2017, Sagar migrated to New Zealand. He completed a Graduate Diploma in Applied Management, which focused his technical knowledge on international business strategy and market segmentation.

Sagar then worked directly in New Zealand retail. This experience provided a practical education in Kiwi consumer psychology, trust-building, and relationship management. He learned that local business owners value direct honesty and simplicity above all else.

After entering the digital marketing agency space in New Zealand, Sagar observed a repeating pattern. Business owners were spending thousands of dollars on websites, hosting, and SEO retainers, but had no idea what they were actually paying for. This inspired him to launch Digital Neighbour in Christchurch as an education-first search agency.

## 4. Director's Strategy: Pranav Tipre
Pranav Tipre joined Digital Neighbour to lead business growth, partnerships, and strategy. He aligned immediately with Sagar's core belief that sales should focus on client needs rather than agency quotas.

Pranav does not believe in selling services for the sake of selling. He focuses on understanding the commercial goals of a business first. He identifies whether a company actually needs SEO, Google Ads, or a new custom website before recommending any service.

This strategy ensures that Digital Neighbour builds sustainable, long-term partnerships. We treat our client relationships as collaborative projects. Pranav's focus on clear negotiation and client strategy has been key to expanding our presence in Auckland and Christchurch.
