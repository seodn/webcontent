## 14. Frequently Asked Questions
*   **What is the difference between Local SEO and AI SEO?**  
    Local SEO focuses on ranking in Google Maps and local keyword search boxes. AI SEO (AEO/GEO) optimizes your site structure and data schemas so conversational search systems like ChatGPT and Siri recommend your business.
*   **Why is website load speed important for SEO?**  
    Website load speed is a critical ranking factor. AI search engines retrieve data in real-time. If your website is slow, the AI crawler will skip your site and recommend a faster competitor. We aim for a sub-1.5 second loading speed.
*   **How do you track SEO success?**  
    We focus on business metrics rather than keyword charts. We track phone calls, contact form submissions, booking completions, and organic traffic growth.
*   **Do you offer month-to-month search retainers?**  
    Yes. We believe trust should be earned every day. We operate on open-term agreements with no locked-in contracts.
*   **Will I need to update my website content for AI search?**
