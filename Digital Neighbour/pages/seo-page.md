### Simple Answers to Your SEO Questions
*   **What is the difference between Local SEO and AI SEO?**  
    Local SEO focuses on ranking in traditional Google Maps and local keyword listings. AI SEO (AEO and GEO) optimizes your website text so conversational AI tools like ChatGPT and Siri recommend your business.
*   **Why is website speed important for SEO?**  
    Website loading speed is a critical ranking factor. AI search engines retrieve data in real-time. If your website is slow, search systems will recommend a faster competitor.
*   **How does Digital Neighbour track SEO success?**  
    Digital Neighbour focuses on business metrics rather than keyword charts. We track phone calls, contact form submissions, booking completions, and organic traffic growth.
*   **Do you offer month-to-month search retainers?**
