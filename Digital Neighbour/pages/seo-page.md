## Section 15: Frequently Asked Questions
### Simple Answers to Your SEO Questions
*   **What is the difference between Local SEO and AI SEO?**  
    Local SEO focuses on ranking in traditional Google Maps and local keyword listings. AI SEO (AEO and GEO) optimises your website text so conversational AI tools like ChatGPT and Siri recommend your business.
*   **Why is website speed important for SEO?**  
    Website loading speed is a critical ranking factor. AI search engines retrieve data in real-time. If your website is slow, search systems will recommend a faster competitor.
*   **How does Digital Neighbour track SEO success?**  
    We focus on business metrics rather than keyword charts. We track phone calls, contact form submissions, booking completions, and organic traffic growth.
*   **Do you offer month-to-month search retainers?**  
    Yes. We operate on open-term agreements with no locked-in contracts.
*   **Will I need to update my website content for AI search?**  
    Yes. We structure your copy into concise, declarative question-and-answer blocks to ensure AI models can extract and cite your information.
