## 3. Foundational Knowledge: Understanding SEO Services
Search engine optimisation is not a single task. It is a combination of specialized techniques designed to establish your search authority.

### Local SEO
Local SEO ensures your business appears when customers search for services near them. This involves optimizing your Google Business Profile, nesting local coordinates in your site code, and connecting your profile to Wikidata nodes. It is essential for trades, medical clinics, and retail stores in Christchurch and Auckland.

### AI SEO (Answer Engine Optimisation)
AI SEO, also known as Generative Engine Optimisation, structures your content to be read by AI web crawlers. We organize your web pages into direct Q&A formats and verify your data signals. This allows platforms like ChatGPT, Google Gemini, and Siri to select your business as a cited answer.

### E-commerce SEO
E-commerce SEO targets buyers looking for products online. We optimize product pages, category hierarchies, and product schema scripts. This displays your inventory, pricing, and stock status directly in search results and AI shopping agents.

### Conversion Rate Optimisation (CRO)
SEO drives visitors to your website, while CRO converts those visitors into leads. We optimize site layout, form fields, and click actions. This ensures that your incoming traffic results in actual inquiries, bookings, and calls.
