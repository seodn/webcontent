# Sagar Bhola & Digital Neighbour

## Internal Team Principles
### How We Think At Digital Neighbour

The way we think is just as important as the work we deliver.

Technology changes.
Google changes.
AI changes.

However, principles remain the same.
Every team member at Digital Neighbour should understand these principles.

#### Educate Before You Sell
One of the biggest reasons Digital Neighbour was started was because many businesses were being sold marketing services without understanding what they were buying.
We do not want to become that company.

If a client does not understand:
- SEO
- Websites
- Hosting
- Google Ads
- AI Search

Then it is our responsibility to educate them.
An educated customer becomes a better customer.

#### Quality Before Speed
Fast work means nothing if it creates problems.
We would rather spend extra time ensuring quality than rush a project and create issues later.

#### Every Client Matters
Whether a client spends $500 per month or $10,000 per month, the planning process remains the same.
Every client deserves attention.
Every client deserves strategy.
Every client deserves quality.

#### Small Details Matter
Many businesses lose leads because:
- Contact forms stop working
- Phone numbers are incorrect
- Emails do not arrive
- Pages break
- Tracking stops working

Small details create large consequences.
This is why we monitor.

#### Think Like A Business Owner
When working on a project, ask: "If this was my business, what would I want?"
That mindset creates better work.

## Digital Neighbour Website Philosophy

Most agencies treat websites as design projects.
We do not.
We believe websites are business assets.

A website should:
- Build trust
- Generate enquiries
- Educate visitors
- Improve visibility
- Convert visitors into customers

A beautiful website that does not convert is not successful.
A website should help a business grow.

### What Makes A Great Website
A great website should:
- Load quickly
- Be mobile-friendly
- Clearly explain services
- Be easy to navigate
- Have strong calls to action
- Demonstrate expertise

- Support SEO

Every website should have a purpose.

## Digital Neighbour Google Ads Philosophy

Google Ads is not a replacement for SEO.
SEO is not a replacement for Google Ads.
They solve different problems.

### When Google Ads Makes Sense
Google Ads may be appropriate when:
- Immediate enquiries are needed
- A new business launches
- Seasonal campaigns are running
- Competition is high
- Fast visibility is required

### When SEO Makes Sense
SEO may be appropriate when:
- Long-term visibility is required
- Organic enquiries are important
- Sustainable growth is desired
- Search presence needs strengthening

### The Best Outcomes
Many businesses benefit from both.
Google Ads can generate visibility today.
SEO can generate visibility tomorrow.

## Digital Neighbour Hosting Philosophy

Many businesses underestimate hosting.
Hosting is not simply a technical service.
Hosting directly impacts:
- Website speed
- User experience
- Security
- Uptime
- Search visibility

### Hosting Analogy
We explain hosting like this:
Your website is your phone. Hosting is your network provider. Your domain is your phone number.
Without all three, communication becomes difficult.

### Monitoring Philosophy
We believe websites should be monitored.
A business owner should not discover problems from customers.

This is why we monitor:
- Website uptime
- Contact forms
- Core functionality
- Security

### Contact Form Testing
One of our strongest beliefs is that contact forms should be tested regularly.
We often explain it like this:
Testing a contact form is like checking whether your business phone is still ringing.
If nobody tests it, nobody knows if it works.
