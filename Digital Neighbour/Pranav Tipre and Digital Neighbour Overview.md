# THE EVOLUTION OF DIGITAL NEIGHBOUR

When Digital Neighbour started in 2022, the company was a full-service digital marketing agency.

The business provided:

- Website design
- Website development
- Website hosting
- SEO
- Google Ads
- Social media marketing
- Branding
- General digital marketing

The objective was simple.

Help businesses wherever they needed support.

As the company grew, Digital Neighbour worked with businesses from many different industries including:

- Mechanics
- Tradies
- Electricians
- Plumbers
- Builders
- Contractors
- Developers
- Dentists
- Healthcare providers
- Physios
- Childcare centres
- Restaurants
- Florists
- Supermarkets
- Security companies
- Tour operators
- Cleaning companies
- Movers
- Beauty businesses
- Property-related businesses

Working across these industries created a unique perspective.

The company began seeing patterns.

Regardless of industry, the businesses achieving the best long-term results generally had three things:

- Strong websites
- Strong search visibility
- Strong organic presence

Over time, Digital Neighbour realised that most of its best results were not coming from social media.

They were not coming from short-term campaigns.

They were coming from search.

The company started reviewing years of work.

When looking back, it became obvious that approximately 80-90% of the most successful projects involved:

- SEO
- Websites
- Organic visibility
- Search rankings

This was a turning point.

Digital Neighbour realised that its true passion was not broad digital marketing.

Its passion was helping businesses become visible where customers are searching.

That insight has driven the company’s evolution towards becoming:

An SEO-first, AI-search-focused, and website-led agency.
