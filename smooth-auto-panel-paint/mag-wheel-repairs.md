## Why Do Your Wheels Need Repairing?
Alloy wheels are easily damaged by everyday driving hazards. At Smooth Auto Panel & Paint, we fix these common issues regularly:

## Wheel Repair vs. Wheel Replacement
We select the smartest, most cost-effective method to fix your vehicle:
*   **Mag Wheel Repair:** Perfect for cosmetic damage like curb rash, scratches, and peeling paint. It is fast, affordable, and keeps your original factory parts.

### Aftercare Guide
## Simple Tips to Protect Your Fresh Wheels
*   **Avoid Harsh Acid Cleaners:** Do not use cheap, acidic wheel cleaners as they will eat into your new clear coat.
*   **Gentle Hand Washing:** Wash your wheels regularly with mild car wash soap and a soft wheel brush to prevent brake dust buildup.
*   **Mind High Curbs:** Take extra care when parallel parking to avoid scraping the freshly painted lip against the concrete.
