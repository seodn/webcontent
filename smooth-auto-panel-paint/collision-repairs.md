### Common Signs of Undetected Post-Crash Damage
Even low-speed fender benders can cause hidden structural damage that compromises your vehicle's safety and passenger protection. Common issues we resolve daily include:
*   **Crinkled Crash Crumple Zones:** Metal crumple zones absorb crash impact. Once bent, they will not protect you in a future collision.
*   **Pillar and Door Realignment:** Damaged A, B, or C-pillars cause doors to bind, allow wind noise, and fail WOF safety checks.
*   **Hidden Chassis Twists:** Twisted underbody frames cause dangerous vehicle handling, heavy tyre wear, and steering pull.
*   **Cracked Structural Weld Joins:** Stress fractures in subframe weld joins weaken the vehicle's structural integrity.

### Choosing Between Insurance Claiming and Private Repairing
Motorists often weigh claiming against paying privately:
*   **Filing an Insurance Claim:** Best for major collision damage, frame twists, or multi-panel scrapes. Your insurer covers the bulk of the cost, and you only pay your pre-set policy excess.
*   **Private Repair Payment:** Best for minor parking scrapes or bumper scuffs where the repair cost is lower than your insurance excess, saving your no-claims bonus.

### Caring for Your Car After Smash Repairs
*   **Do Not Use High-Pressure Washes:** Avoid high-pressure wand washes and automated brush washes for the first 30 days to protect clear coats.
*   **Gentle Hand Wash Only:** Wash with fresh water and soft microfiber mitts. Avoid abrasive household sponges.
*   **Rinse Tree Sap Immediately:** Wash off tree sap and bird droppings immediately to prevent paint etching and clear coat damage.
