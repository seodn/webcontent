### Common Scenarios
## Why Hidden Damage Matters After an Accident
Even minor fender benders can cause hidden damage that compromises your safety. We check and resolve these common issues daily:
*   **Bent Crumple Zones:** Structural crumple zones absorb crash impact. Once bent, they will not protect you properly in another accident.
*   **Misaligned Doors & Pillars:** Damaged pillars cause doors to stick, let in wind noise, and fail WOF checks.
*   **Hidden Chassis Twists:** A twisted frame causes dangerous handling, heavy tyre wear, and steering pull.
*   **Cracked Welds:** Stress cracks in your car's underbody subframe weaken its overall strength.

### Service Comparison
## Insurance Claiming vs. Private Repair Payment
We help you weigh your options based on the extent of the damage:
*   **Filing an Insurance Claim:** Best for major collision damage, frame twists, or multi-panel scrapes. Your insurer covers the bulk of the cost, and you only pay your policy excess.
*   **Private Repair Payment:** Best for minor scrapes or bumper scuffs where the repair cost is lower than your insurance excess, saving your no-claims bonus.

### Aftercare Guide
## Caring for Your Car After Smash Repairs
Proper aftercare helps maintain your vehicle’s paint finish, panel alignment, and long-term durability after smash repairs. Following these simple care steps can help protect newly repaired and painted surfaces.
*   **Do Not Use High-Pressure Washes:** Avoid high-pressure wand washes and automated brush washes for the first 30 days to protect clear coats.
*   **Gentle Hand Wash Only:** Wash with fresh water and soft microfibre mitts. Avoid abrasive household sponges.
*   **Rinse Tree Sap Immediately:** Wash off tree sap and bird droppings quickly to prevent paint damage.
