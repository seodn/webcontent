### Restoring Your Vehicle’s Safety and Value
Collisions, parking lot bumps, and road debris cause immediate damage that lowers your vehicle’s value and compromises safety. Common issues we resolve daily include:
*   **Cracked or Sagging Bumpers:** Plastic bumpers lose structural strength when cracked and will not protect you in a future crash.
*   **Failed WOF Structural Rust:** Rust weakens vehicle crumple zones and underbodies, resulting in immediate Warrant of Fitness failure.
*   **Mismatched Paint & Scratches:** Deeper paint scratches expose bare sheet metal, leading to rapid rust spreading under Auckland's damp conditions.
*   **Chassis Misalignment:** Severe accidents bend vehicle frames, causing uneven tyre wear and dangerous vehicle handling.

### Choosing the Right Repair Approach
When restoring dented panels, we select the safest, most cost-effective method:
*   **Paintless Dent Removal (PDR):** Used for minor parking dings and hail damage where the paint surface is unbroken. PDR is faster and cheaper because it preserves original factory paint.
*   **Traditional Panel Beating:** Required for deeper dents, creases, and collision impacts. We pull the metal back, fill imperfections, prime, and respray for a factory-fresh look.

### Caring for Your New Paint Work
*   **Avoid Automated Car Washes:** Do not use high-pressure brush car washes for the first 30 days to let the clear coat settle.
*   **Gentle Hand Washing:** Wash your vehicle using fresh water, a soft microfiber mitt, and pH-neutral automotive soap.
*   **Immediate Debris Removal:** Rinse off bird droppings and tree sap immediately with low-pressure water to prevent paint etching.
