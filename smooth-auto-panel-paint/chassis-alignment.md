### About Us
## Understanding Chassis Alignment & Structural Repairs

Chassis alignment is the process of restoring a vehicle's structural frame after it has been damaged in an accident or impact. Even minor frame distortion can affect steering, suspension performance, tyre wear, and overall vehicle safety if left uncorrected.

Using specialised measuring systems and frame-straightening equipment, chassis alignment helps return the vehicle to manufacturer specifications. This ensures critical structural components are correctly positioned and allows the vehicle to perform as intended on the road.

### Service Comparison
## Frame Repair vs. Writing Off the Vehicle
We help you and your insurer decide the best path forward after a heavy crash:
*   **Frame Repair:** Perfect when the damage is isolated and the cost of repair is significantly lower than the vehicle's market value.
*   **Total Loss (Write-Off):** Necessary when the chassis is twisted beyond safe repair limits, or if the cost of replacing the crumpled structural parts is too high.

## Simple Tips After a Major Repair
Proper aftercare helps ensure your vehicle continues to perform safely after structural repairs. Monitoring tyre wear, steering feel, and suspension performance can help identify any issues and maintain long-term driving confidence.
